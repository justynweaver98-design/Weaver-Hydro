Our Story

Built on Integrity. Driven by Service. Focused on Quality.

“Our name is on every project we complete, and that's a responsibility we take seriously.”

My name is Justyn Weaver, and I want to personally thank you for taking the time to learn more about our company.

As a lifelong resident of Middle Georgia, I have always believed that a person's reputation is built through honesty, hard work, and the way they treat others.

A Journey of Service

Before founding Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care, I proudly served the citizens of Middle Georgia in law enforcement for more than a decade.

Following a career-ending injury, I was medically retired from law enforcement. While that chapter came to an unexpected end, it opened the door to a new opportunity—to continue serving my community in a different way.

The profession changed. The commitment to serving others never did.

Family Is Our Foundation

Family is the heart of everything we do. The values I strive to teach my children—honesty, kindness, responsibility, humility, and respect—are the same values that shape this company.

When you choose Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care, you're supporting a local, family-owned business that believes relationships matter more than transactions.
