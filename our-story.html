Meet the Owner

Justyn Weaver

Welcome to Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care, LLC. I'm Justyn Weaver, and I appreciate you taking the time to learn more about our company.

As a lifelong resident of Middle Georgia, I've always believed that a person's reputation is built through honesty, hard work, and the way they treat others. Those values were shaped by my family, strengthened through years of public service, and continue to guide every decision I make today.

Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care was founded on a simple idea: provide dependable service, deliver quality workmanship, and treat every customer with the same respect I'd expect for my own family.

Our business isn't just about growing grass—it's about growing trust.
A Journey of Service

The profession changed. The commitment to serving others never did.

Before founding Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care, I proudly served the citizens of Middle Georgia in law enforcement for more than a decade.

Following a career-ending injury, I was medically retired from law enforcement. While that chapter came to an unexpected end, it opened the door to a new opportunity—to continue serving homeowners, builders, businesses, and property owners in a different way.

Family Is Our Foundation

A family name, a local reputation, and a responsibility we take personally.

The values I strive to teach my children—honesty, kindness, responsibility, humility, and respect—are the same values that shape this company.

When you choose Weaver Hydroseeding & Lawn Care, you're supporting a local family-owned business that values relationships more than transactions and believes every customer deserves professionalism and respect.
